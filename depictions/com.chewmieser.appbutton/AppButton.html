Beta - iOS9

AppButton

AppButton puts your app switcher (and now favorited apps) at the touch of a button, acessible from anywhere!

Swiping downwards opens your multitasking tray. Swiping upwards opens your favorites tray.

Drag the button to your desired app and release! Move the button by holding until it turns while and drag to your desired location.
